# ARTS
**ARTS** = **A**lgorithm + **R**eview + **T**ip + **S**hare

## Algorithm
At least 1 algorithm per week
每周至少做一个 Leetcode 的算法题

## Review
Read and comment on at least 1 technical article
阅读并点评至少一篇英文技术文章

## Tip
Get at least 1 technical tip 
学习至少一个技术技巧

## Share
Share at least 1 technical article
至少分享一篇有观点和思考的技术文章
